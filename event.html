Alkmaar Cheese Market

The Alkmaar Cheese Market is the most iconic event in the city. Since 1365, Waagplein has been the stage for Dutch cheese trading, where wheels of Gouda and Edam are weighed, carried, and sold in a colorful spectacle. Dressed in traditional white uniforms with straw hats, the cheese porters bring history to life in front of thousands of visitors.
